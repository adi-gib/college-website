University Overview
Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private deemed university established in 1984 by Dr. G. Viswanathan. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and integrated programs and has campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Amaravati, Bhopal, Bengaluru, and Mauritius.
🚀
Student-Centric Learning
VIT pioneered the Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS), allowing students to choose courses, faculty, and schedules. This promotes personalized learning and interdisciplinary exploration.
🎯
Student Life & Clubs
VIT hosts student chapters like IEEE, ACM, ASME, AIESEC, and SAE International. Students actively participate in robotics, aero design, coding clubs, and hackathons.
🌐
Global Recognition
VIT is globally ranked including QS World Ranking 691 (2026) and holds A++ NAAC accreditation. It is among the top private engineering institutions in India.

RIVIERA
Riviera – VIT Vellore
📅 February 🌍 International 🏅 Guinness Record

Riviera is the flagship international sports and cultural festival of VIT Vellore, conducted annually in February. Spanning four days, Riviera 2025 hosted over 45,000 students from 80+ universities across 26 countries. The festival includes sports tournaments, cultural competitions, pro-shows, and social awareness events, and has achieved both ISO certification and a Guinness World Record.

VIBRANCE
Vibrance – VIT Chennai
📅 Feb–March 🎨 Cultural ⚽ Sports

Vibrance is the flagship cultural and sports festival of VIT Chennai, held in late February or early March. This four-day event attracts 22,000+ students including international participants, showcasing art, music, dance, and athletics. It is known for its student-led organization, inclusivity, and dynamic energy.
